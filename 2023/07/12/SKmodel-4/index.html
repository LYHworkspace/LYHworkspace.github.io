2. First-step RSB

\[ \sum_{\alpha<\beta} q_{\alpha \beta} S^\alpha S^\beta=\frac{1}{2}\left\{q_0\left(\sum_\alpha^n S^\alpha\right)^2+\left(q_1-q_0\right) \sum_{\text {block }}^{n / m_1}\left(\sum_{\alpha \in \text { block }}^{m_1} S^\alpha\right)^2-n q_1\right\} \] 以及 \[ \lim _{n \rightarrow 0} \frac{1}{n} \sum_{\alpha \neq \beta} q_{\alpha \beta}^2=\lim _{n \rightarrow 0} \frac{1}{n}\left\{n^2 q_0^2+\frac{n}{m_1} m_1^2\left(q_1^2-q_0^2\right)-n q_1^2\right\}=\left(m_1-1\right) q_1^2-m_1 q_0^2 \]

因此 \[ \begin{aligned} \beta f_{1 \mathrm{RSB}}= & \frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}\Big\{ \left(m_1-1\right) q_1^2-m_1 q_0^2+2 q_1-1\Big\}\\ & +\frac{\beta J_0}{2} m^2-\log 2 -\frac{1}{m_1} \int \mathrm{D} u \log \int \mathrm{D} v \cosh ^{m_1} \Xi \end{aligned} \] 其中 \[ \Xi= \beta\left(J \sqrt{q_0} u+J \sqrt{q_1-q_0} v+J_0 m+h\right) \]
