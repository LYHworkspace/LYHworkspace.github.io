SKmodel-3 | 复本对称解的稳定性分析

发表于 2023-07-12 分类于 Note ， SpinGlass

Abstract
主要参考了 Nishimori 的 Statistical Physics of Spin Glasses and Information Processing An Introduction 第二章以及 Haozhe Shan 的笔记 Replica calculations for the SK model.

Hessian 矩阵

考虑 \(h=0\) 的情况，并且令 \[ y^{\alpha \beta} \equiv \beta J q_{\alpha \beta}, \quad x ^\alpha \equiv \sqrt{\beta J_0} m_{\alpha} \] 自由能写为 \[ \begin{aligned} [f]= -\frac{\beta J^2}{4} -& \lim _{n \rightarrow 0} \frac{1}{\beta n}\left\{-\sum_{\alpha<\beta} \frac{1}{2}\left(y^{\alpha \beta}\right)^2-\sum_\alpha \frac{1}{2}\left(x^\alpha\right)^2\right. \\ & +\left. \log \operatorname{Tr} \exp \left(\beta J \sum_{\alpha<\beta} y^{\alpha \beta} S^\alpha S^\beta+\sqrt{\beta J_0} \sum_\alpha x^\alpha S^\alpha\right) \right\} \end{aligned} \] 为了检查复本对称解的稳定性，考虑将复本对称解加入小偏差 \[ x^\alpha = x + \epsilon^\alpha,\quad y^{\alpha \beta} = y + \eta^{\alpha \beta} \] 将上式最后一项展开至二阶有 \[ \begin{aligned} &\log \operatorname{Tr} \exp \left(\beta J \sum_{\alpha<\beta} y^{\alpha \beta} S^\alpha S^\beta+\sqrt{\beta J_0} \sum_\alpha x^\alpha S^\alpha\right)\\ &=\log \operatorname{Tr} \exp \left( L_0 +\beta J \sum_{\alpha<\beta} \eta^{\alpha \beta} S^\alpha S^\beta+\sqrt{\beta J_0} \sum_\alpha \epsilon^\alpha S^\alpha\right)\\ &\approx \log \operatorname{Tr} \mathrm{e}^{L_0}+\frac{\beta J_0}{2} \sum_{\alpha \beta} \epsilon^\alpha \epsilon^\beta\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle_{L_0}+\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{2} \sum_{\alpha<\beta} \sum_{\gamma<\delta} \eta^{\alpha \beta} \eta^{\gamma \delta}\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta S^\gamma S^\delta\right\rangle_{L_0} \\ & -\frac{\beta J_0}{2} \sum_{\alpha \beta} \epsilon^\alpha \epsilon^\beta\left\langle S^\alpha\right\rangle_{L_0}\left\langle S^\beta\right\rangle_{L_0}-\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{2} \sum_{\alpha<\beta} \sum_{\gamma<\delta} \eta^{\alpha \beta} \eta^{\gamma \delta}\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle_{L_0}\left\langle S^\gamma S^\delta\right\rangle_{L_0} \\ & -\beta J \sqrt{\beta J_0} \sum_\delta \sum_{\alpha<\beta} \epsilon^\delta \eta^{\alpha \beta}\left\langle S^\delta\right\rangle_{L_0}\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle_{L_0} \\ & +\beta J \sqrt{\beta J_0} \sum_\delta \sum_{\alpha<\beta} \epsilon^\delta \eta^{\alpha \beta}\left\langle S^\delta S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle_{L_0} \end{aligned} \] 整理二阶项 \[ \begin{aligned} \Delta &\equiv \frac{1}{2} \sum_{\alpha \beta}\left\{\delta_{\alpha \beta}-\beta J_0\left(\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle_{L_0}-\left\langle S^\alpha\right\rangle_{L_0}\left\langle S^\beta\right\rangle_{L_0}\right)\right\} \epsilon^\alpha \epsilon^\beta\\ &+\beta J \sqrt{\beta J_0} \sum_\delta \sum_{\alpha<\beta}\left(\left\langle S^\delta\right\rangle_{L_0}\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle_{L_0}-\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta S^\delta\right\rangle_{L_0}\right) \epsilon^\delta \eta^{\alpha \beta} \\ &+\frac{1}{2} \sum_{\alpha<\beta} \sum_{\gamma<\delta}\left\{\delta_{(\alpha \beta)(\delta \gamma)}-\beta^2 J^2\left(\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta S^\gamma S^\delta\right\rangle_{L_0} -\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle_{L_0}\left\langle S^\gamma S^\delta\right\rangle_{L_0}\right)\right\} \eta^{\alpha \beta} \eta^{\gamma \delta} \end{aligned} \] 写出Hessian矩阵，\(\epsilon\) 的二阶项系数有两个不同的值： \[ \begin{aligned} & G_{\alpha \alpha}=1-\beta J_0\left(1-\left\langle S^\alpha\right\rangle^2\right) \equiv A \\ & G_{\alpha \beta}=-\beta J_0\left(\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle-\left\langle S^\alpha\right\rangle^2\right) \equiv B \end{aligned} \] \(\eta\) 的二阶项系数有三个不同的值： \[ \begin{aligned} & G_{(\alpha \beta)(\alpha \beta)}=1-\beta^2 J^2\left(1-\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle^2\right) \equiv P \\ & G_{(\alpha \beta)(\alpha \gamma)}=-\beta^2 J^2\left(\left\langle S^\beta S^\gamma\right\rangle-\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle^2\right) \equiv Q \\ & G_{(\alpha \beta)(\gamma \delta)}=-\beta^2 J^2\left(\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta S^\gamma S^\delta\right\rangle-\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle^2\right) \equiv R \end{aligned} \] \(\epsilon\) 和 \(\eta\) 两种不同的交叉项： \[ \begin{aligned} & G_{\alpha(\alpha \beta)}=\beta J \sqrt{\beta J_0}\left(\left\langle S^\alpha\right\rangle\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle-\left\langle S^\beta\right\rangle\right) \equiv C \\ & G_{\gamma(\alpha \beta)}=\beta J \sqrt{\beta J_0}\left(\left\langle S^\gamma\right\rangle\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle-\left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta S^\gamma\right\rangle\right) \equiv D \end{aligned} \] 上面出现的各种期望可以用复本对称解来评估 \[ \begin{aligned} & \left\langle S^\alpha\right\rangle \equiv m = \int \mathrm{D} z \tanh \beta \tilde{H}(z)\\ & \left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta\right\rangle \equiv q = \int \mathrm{D} z \tanh ^2 \beta \tilde{H}(z) \\ & \left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta S^\gamma\right\rangle \equiv t = \int \mathrm{D} z \tanh ^3 \beta \tilde{H}(z) \\ & \left\langle S^\alpha S^\beta S^\gamma S^\delta\right\rangle \equiv r = \int \mathrm{D} z \tanh ^4 \beta \tilde{H}(z) \end{aligned} \]

Hessian矩阵的特征值

特征方程为 \[ G \boldsymbol{\mu} = \lambda \boldsymbol{\mu} \] 其中特征向量为 \[ \boldsymbol{\mu}=\left(\begin{array}{c} \left\{\epsilon^\alpha\right\} \\ \left\{\eta^{\alpha \beta}\right\} \end{array}\right)=\left(\begin{array}{c} \left\{\epsilon^1, \cdots, \epsilon^n\right\} \\ \left\{\eta^{12}, \cdots, \eta^{n-1,n} \right\} \end{array}\right) \] 特征向量有三种不同的类型

  • 第一种为 \(\epsilon^\alpha=a, \eta^{\alpha \beta}=b\)
    此时Hessian矩阵的第一行为 \[ (A,B,\cdots,B,C,\cdots,C,D,\cdots,D) \]
