SK model 2 | 复本对称解

发表于 2023-07-12 分类于 Note ， SpinGlass

Abstract
主要参考了 Nishimori 的 Statistical Physics of Spin Glasses and Information Processing An Introduction 第二章以及 Haozhe Shan 的笔记 Replica calculations for the SK model.

\[ \begin{equation} [F]=-T[\log Z]=-T\int \prod_{(ij)}\mathrm{d}J_{ij}P(J_{ij})\log Z \end{equation} \]

1. Replica trick

\([F]\) 中的 \(\log Z\) 对 \(\boldsymbol{J}\) 的依赖关系是十分复杂的，我们无法直接求 \([\log Z]\). 可以利用如下一个数学技巧（称为复本技巧，replica trick）将对 \(\log Z\) 的构型平均转移到对 \(Z^n\) 的构型平均（称为复本平均，replica average）

\[ \begin{equation} [\log Z]=\lim_{n\to 0}\frac{[Z^n]-1}{n} \end{equation} \]

利用复本技巧，我们可以写出配分函数的复本平均为

\[ \begin{equation} \left[Z^n\right]=\int \prod_{i<j} d J_{i j} P\left(J_{i j}\right)\mathrm{~~Tr~} \exp \left(\beta \sum_{i<j} J_{i j} \sum_{\alpha=1}^n S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha+\beta h \sum_{i=1}^N \sum_{\alpha=1}^n S_i^\alpha\right) \end{equation} \]

其中，\(\alpha\)称为复本指标（replica index），\(\mathrm{Tr~}=\sum_{\left\{\mathbf{s}^\alpha, \mathbf{s}^\beta, \ldots, \mathbf{s}^n\right\}}\). 将 \(P\left(J_{i j}\right)\) 的表达式

\[ \begin{equation} P\left(J_{i j}\right)=\frac{1}{J} \sqrt{\frac{N}{2 \pi}} \exp \left\{-\frac{N}{2 J^2}\left(J_{i j}-\frac{J_0}{N}\right)^2\right\} \end{equation} \]

代入复本平均中，然后进行一个看不懂的积分，得

\[ \begin{equation} \left[Z^n\right]=C_1 \mathrm{~~Tr~} \exp \left\{\frac{1}{N} \sum_{i<j}\left(\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 J^2 \sum_{\alpha, \beta} S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha S_i^\beta S_j^\beta+\beta J_0 \sum_\alpha S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha\right)+\beta h \sum_{i=1}^N \sum_{\alpha=1}^n S_i^\alpha\right\} \end{equation} \]

利用如下技巧

\[ \begin{equation} \sum_{\alpha, \beta} S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha S_i^\beta S_j^\beta=2 \sum_{\alpha<\beta} S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha S_i^\beta S_j^\beta+\sum_\alpha\left(S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha\right)^2=2 \sum_{\alpha<\beta} S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha S_i^\beta S_j^\beta+n \end{equation} \]

将指数部分改写为

\[ \begin{equation} \exp \left\{\frac{1}{N} \sum_{i<j}\left(\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 J^2\left(2 \sum_{\alpha<\beta} S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha S_i^\beta S_j^\beta+n\right)+\beta J_0 \sum_\alpha S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha\right)+\beta h \sum_{i=1}^N \sum_{\alpha=1}^n S_i^\alpha\right\} \end{equation} \]

注意到指数上第一项中的求和号中含有与 \(i\)、 \(j\) 无关的项 \(\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 J^2n\)，并且求和 \(\sum_{i<j}\) 共有 \(N(N-1)/2\) 项，因此将常数项提到前面，改写为

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \left[Z^n\right]=&C_1 \exp \left(\frac{(N-1) \beta^2 J^2 n}{4}\right) \\ &\mathrm{Tr~} \exp \left\{\frac{1}{N} \sum_{i<j}\left(\beta^2 J^2 \sum_{\alpha<\beta} S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha S_i^\beta S_j^\beta+\beta J_0 \sum_\alpha S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha\right)+\beta h \sum_{i=1}^N \sum_{\alpha=1}^n S_i^\alpha\right\} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

并且，根据一个技巧 \(\left(\sum_iA_i\right)^2=\sum_iA_i^2+\sum_{i\neq j}A_iA_j=\sum_iA_i^2+2\sum_{i< j}A_iA_j\)，即

\[ \begin{equation} \sum_{i< j}A_iA_j=\frac{1}{2}\left(\left(\sum_iA_i\right)^2-\sum_iA_i^2\right) \end{equation} \]

可以分别改写

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i<j} \beta^2 J^2 \sum_{\alpha<\beta} S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha S_i^\beta S_j^\beta & =\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{2 N}\left(\sum_{\alpha<\beta}\left(\sum_i S_i^\alpha S_i^\beta\right)^2-\sum_i \sum_{\alpha<\beta} (S_i^\alpha)^2 (S_i^\beta)^2\right) \\ & =\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{2 N} \sum_{\alpha<\beta}\left(\sum_i S_i^\alpha S_i^\beta\right)^2-\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{2 N} \sum_i \sum_{\alpha<\beta} 1 \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

和

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \frac{\beta J_0}{N} \sum_{i<j} \sum_\alpha S_i^\alpha S_j^\alpha&=\frac{\beta J_0}{2 N}\left(\sum_\alpha\left(\sum_i S_i^\alpha\right)^2 -\sum_i \sum_{\alpha} (S_i^\alpha)^2 \right) \\ &=\frac{\beta J_0}{2 N} \sum_\alpha\left(\sum_i S_i^\alpha\right)^2-\frac{\beta J_0}{2 N} \sum_i \sum_\alpha 1 \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

忽略掉上两式中的常数项，并且根据大 \(N\) 极限下的近似 \(\exp \left(\frac{(N-1) \beta^2 J^2 n}{4}\right)\approx \exp \left(\frac{N \beta^2 J^2 n}{4}\right)\)，有

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \left[Z^n\right]&=C_2 \exp \left(\frac{N \beta^2 J^2 n}{4}\right)\\ &=\mathrm{Tr~} \exp \left\{\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{2 N} \sum_{\alpha<\beta}\left(\sum_i S_i^\alpha S_i^\beta\right)^2+\frac{\beta J_0}{2 N} \sum_\alpha\left(\sum_i S_i^\alpha\right)^2+\beta h \sum_{i=1}^N \sum_{\alpha=1}^n S_i^\alpha\right\} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

再利用如下变换（称为 Hubbard-Stratonovich 变换）将 \(\left(\sum_i S_i^\alpha S_i^\beta\right)^2\) 和 \(\left(\sum_i S_i^\alpha\right)^2\) 线性化

\[ \begin{equation} \exp \left(\frac{y^2}{2}\right)=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \frac{\mathrm{d} x}{\sqrt{2 \pi}} \exp \left(-\frac{x^2}{2}\right) \exp (x y) \end{equation} \]

令 \(x:=\beta J\sqrt{N}q_{\alpha\beta}\)，\(y:=\beta J\sum_i S_i^\alpha S_i^\beta/\sqrt{N}\)，有

\[ \begin{equation} \exp \frac{\beta^2 J^2}{2 N}\left(\sum_i S_i^\alpha S_i^\beta\right)^2 =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \frac{\mathrm{d} q_{\alpha \beta}}{\sqrt{2 \pi}} \exp \left(-\beta^2 J^2 N \frac{q_{\alpha \beta}^2}{2}+\beta^2 J^2 q_{\alpha \beta} \sum_i S_i^\alpha S_i^\beta\right) \end{equation} \]

令 \(x:=\sqrt{\beta JN}m_{\alpha}\)，\(y:=\sqrt{\beta J/N}\sum_i S_i^\alpha\)，有

\[ \begin{equation} \exp \frac{\beta J_0}{2 N}\left(\sum_i S_i^\alpha\right)^2 =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \frac{\mathrm{d} m_\alpha}{\sqrt{2 \pi}} \exp \left(-\beta J_0 N m_\alpha^2+\beta J_0 m_\alpha \sum_i S_i^\alpha\right) \end{equation} \]

其中，忽略了积分号前面的常数（由 \(\mathrm{d}x\)带来的）. \(\left[Z^n\right]\) 继续改写为

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \left[Z^n\right]=&\;C_3 \exp \left(\frac{N \beta^2 J^2 n}{4}\right) \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \prod_{\alpha<\beta} \mathrm{d}q_{\alpha \beta} \prod_\alpha \mathrm{d}m_\alpha\\ &\mathrm{Tr~} \exp \left(-\frac{\beta^2 J^2 N}{2} \sum_{\alpha<\beta} q_{\alpha \beta}^2-\frac{\beta J_0 N}{2} \sum_\alpha m_\alpha^2\right) \\ &\exp \left(\beta^2 J^2 \sum_{\alpha<\beta} q_{\alpha \beta} \sum_i S_i^\alpha S_i^\beta+\beta J_0 \sum_\alpha m_\alpha \sum_i S_i^\alpha\right) \exp \left(\beta h \sum_{i=1}^N \sum_{\alpha=1}^n S_i^\alpha\right)\\ =&\;C_3 \exp \left(\frac{N \beta^2 J^2 n}{4}\right) \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \prod_{\alpha<\beta} \mathrm{d}q_{\alpha \beta} \prod_\alpha \mathrm{d}m_\alpha\;\;\exp \left(-\frac{\beta^2 J^2 N}{2} \sum_{\alpha<\beta} q_{\alpha \beta}^2-\frac{\beta J_0 N}{2} \sum_\alpha m_\alpha^2\right) \\ &\mathrm{Tr~}\exp \left(\beta^2 J^2 \sum_{\alpha<\beta} q_{\alpha \beta} \sum_i S_i^\alpha S_i^\beta+\beta \sum_\alpha\left(J_0 m_\alpha+h\right) \sum_i S_i^\alpha\right) \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

将最后一项指数上的求和号拿下来成为连乘号，即

\[ \begin{equation} \prod_{i=1}^N \mathrm{Tr~}\exp \left(\beta^2 J^2 \sum_{\alpha<\beta} q_{\alpha \beta} S_i^\alpha S_i^\beta+\beta \sum_\alpha\left(J_0 m_\alpha+h\right) S_i^\alpha\right) \end{equation} \]

令

\[ \begin{equation} L:=\beta^2 J^2 \sum_{\alpha<\beta} q_{\alpha \beta} S_i^\alpha S_i^\beta+\beta \sum_\alpha\left(J_0 m_\alpha+h\right) S_i^\alpha \end{equation} \]

考虑到连乘中的每一项都是对相同的变量 \(\left\{\mathbf{s}^\alpha, \mathbf{s}^\beta, \ldots, \mathbf{s}^n\right\}\) 的相同取值 \(\{\pm 1\}\) 进行的，与 \(i\) 无关，因此

\[ \begin{equation} \prod_{i=1}^N \mathrm{Tr~}\exp \left(L\right)=\Big(\mathrm{Tr~}\exp \left(L\right)\Big)^N=\exp \Big\{ N\log\Big(\mathrm{Tr~}\exp \left(L\right)\Big) \Big\} \end{equation} \]

\(\left[Z^n\right]\) 改写为

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \left[Z^n\right] =&C_4 \exp \left(\frac{N \beta^2 J^2 n}{4}\right) \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \prod_{\alpha<\beta} d q_{\alpha \beta} \prod_\alpha d m_\alpha \\ & \exp \left\{-\frac{\beta^2 J^2 N}{2} \sum_{\alpha<\beta} q_{\alpha \beta}^2-\frac{\beta J_0 N}{2} \sum_\alpha m_\alpha^2+N\log\Big(\mathrm{Tr~}\exp \left(L\right)\Big)\right\} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

接下来用鞍点近似法（method of steepest descent）来考虑这个积分. 上式的积分中，最后一项指数上与 \(N\) 成正比，在大 \(N\)极限下，积分结果由指数上的宗量的最大值决定. 即

\[ \begin{equation} \left[Z^n\right]=C_4 \exp \left\{\frac{N \beta^2 J^2 n}{4}-\frac{\beta^2 J^2 N}{2} \sum_{\alpha<\beta}\left(q_{\alpha \beta}^{\star}\right)^2-\frac{\beta J_0 N}{2} \sum_\alpha\left(m_\alpha^{\star}\right)^2+N \log \mathrm{Tr~} \exp (L)\right\} \end{equation} \]

其中，\(\left\{q_{\alpha \beta}^{\star}, m_\alpha^{\star}\right\}=\arg \max _{\left\{q_{\alpha \beta}, m_\alpha\right\}} E\)

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} E:=-\frac{\beta^2 J^2 N}{2} \sum_{\alpha<\beta} q_{\alpha \beta}^2-\frac{\beta J_0 N}{2} \sum_\alpha m_\alpha^2+N \log \mathrm{Tr~}\exp (L) \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

令 \(\frac{\partial}{\partial q_{\alpha\beta}}E=0\)，得

\[ \begin{equation} q_{\alpha \beta}^{\star}=\frac{1}{\beta^2 J^2} \frac{\partial}{\partial q_{\alpha \beta}} \log \mathrm{Tr~} \exp (L)=\frac{1}{\beta^2 J^2} \frac{\mathrm{Tr~} \exp (L) \beta^2 J^2}{\mathrm{Tr~}\exp (L)} S^\alpha S^\beta=\frac{\mathrm{Tr~} S^\alpha S^\beta \exp (L)}{\mathrm{Tr~}\exp (L)} \end{equation} \]

令 \(\frac{\partial}{\partial m_{\alpha}}E=0\)，得

\[ \begin{equation} m_\alpha^{\star}=\frac{1}{\beta J_0} \frac{\partial}{\partial m_\alpha} \log \mathrm{Tr~} \exp (L)=\frac{1}{\beta J_0}\frac{\mathrm{Tr~} \exp (L)\beta J_0}{\mathrm{Tr~} \exp (L)} S^\alpha=\frac{\mathrm{Tr~} S^\alpha \exp (L)}{\mathrm{Tr~} \exp (L)} \end{equation} \]

再将 \(\left[Z^n\right]\) 改写为

\[ \begin{equation} \left[Z^n\right]=C_4 \exp \left\{N n\left(\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}-\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{2 n} \sum_{\alpha<\beta}\left(q_{\alpha \beta}^{\star}\right)^2-\frac{\beta J_0}{2 n} \sum_\alpha\left(m_\alpha^{\star}\right)^2+\frac{1}{n} \log\mathrm{Tr~} \exp (L)\right)\right\} \end{equation} \]

考虑 \(n\to 0\) 的极限，此时指数上的宗量趋于零（这里其实是一个“\(0\cdot\infty\)”极限未定式，应当讨论无穷的阶数. 但是现在先姑且认为 \(0\cdot\infty=0\)），利用Maclaurin展开得

\[ \begin{equation} \left[Z^n\right] \approx 1+N n\left\{\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}-\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{2 n} \sum_{\alpha<\beta}\left(q_{\alpha \beta}^{\star}\right)^2-\frac{\beta J_0}{2 n} \sum_\alpha\left(m_\alpha^{\star}\right)^2+\frac{1}{n} \log \mathrm{Tr~} \exp (L)\right\} \end{equation} \]

至此，\(\left[\log Z\right]\) 可以写为

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} [\log Z] & =N\lim _{n \rightarrow 0} \frac{\left[Z^n\right]-1}{N n} \\ & =N\lim _{n \rightarrow 0}\left\{\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}-\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{2 n} \sum_{\alpha<\beta}\left(q_{\alpha \beta}^{\star}\right)^2-\frac{\beta J_0}{2 n} \sum_\alpha\left(m_\alpha^{\star}\right)^2+\frac{1}{n} \log \mathrm{Tr~} \exp (L)\right\} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

2. Replica Symmetry Solution

根据复本对称假设 \(\forall \alpha, \beta: q_{\alpha \beta}=q, m_\alpha=m\)，上式可以写为

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} [\log Z] & =N\lim _{n \rightarrow 0}\left\{\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}-\frac{\beta^2 J^2(n-1)}{4} q^2-\frac{\beta J_0}{2} m^2+\frac{1}{n} \log \mathrm{Tr~} \exp \left(L*\right)\right\} \\ & =N\left\{\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}\left(1+q^2\right)-\frac{\beta J_0}{2} m^2+\lim _{n \rightarrow 0} \frac{1}{n} \log \mathrm{Tr~} \exp \left(L*\right)\right\} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

其中第一步用到了求和 \(\sum_{\alpha<\beta}\) 共有 \(n(n-1)/2\) 项，并且

\[ \begin{equation} L*:=L(q_{\alpha \beta}=q, m_\alpha=m)=\beta^2 J^2 q \sum_{\alpha<\beta} S^\alpha S^\beta+\beta\left(J_0 m+h\right) \sum_\alpha S^\alpha \end{equation} \]

考虑最后一项

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} &\frac{1}{n} \log \mathrm{Tr~} \exp \left(L*\right)\\ &=\frac{1}{n} \log \mathrm{Tr~}\exp \left(\beta^2 J^2 q \sum_{\alpha<\beta} S^\alpha S^\beta+\beta\left(J_0 m+h\right) \sum_\alpha S^\alpha\right)\\ &=\frac{1}{n} \log \mathrm{Tr~}\exp \left\{\frac{1}{2}\beta^2 J^2 q \left(\sum_{\alpha} S^\alpha \right)^2-\frac{1}{2}\beta^2 J^2 qn+\beta\left(J_0 m+h\right) \sum_\alpha S^\alpha\right\}\\ &=\frac{1}{n} \log \left\{ \exp\left( -\frac{\beta^2 J^2 qn}{2} \right) \mathrm{Tr~}\exp \left[ \frac{1}{2}\beta^2 J^2 q \left(\sum_{\alpha} S^\alpha \right)^2+\beta\left(J_0 m+h\right) \sum_\alpha S^\alpha \right] \right\} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

对于\(\exp \left[ \frac{1}{2}\beta^2 J^2 q \left(\sum_{\alpha} S^\alpha \right)^2\right]\)，再次使用 Hubbard-Stratonovich 变换对其线性化：
令\(x:=\hat{z}\beta J\sqrt{q}\)，\(y:=\beta J\sqrt{q}\sum_\alpha S^\alpha\)，有

\[ \begin{equation} \exp \left[ \frac{1}{2}\beta^2 J^2 q \left(\sum_{\alpha} S^\alpha \right)^2\right]=\int_C \mathrm{d}\hat{z}\sqrt{\frac{\beta^2 J^2 q}{2\pi}}\exp\left(-\frac{\hat{z}^2}{2}\beta^2 J^2 q\right)\exp\left(\beta^2 J^2 q\hat{z}\sum_{\alpha} S^\alpha\right) \end{equation} \]

注意到 \[ \begin{equation} \mathrm{d}\hat{z}\sqrt{\frac{\beta^2 J^2 q}{2\pi}}\exp\left(-\frac{\hat{z}^2}{2}\beta^2 J^2 q\right)=\mathrm{d}\hat{z}p(\hat{z}),\quad p(\hat{z}):=\sqrt{\frac{\beta^2 J^2 q}{2\pi}}\exp\left(-\frac{\hat{z}^2}{2}\beta^2 J^2 q\right) \end{equation} \] 即 \(\hat{z}\) 是一个服从 \(p(\hat{z})\) 的高斯变量，将其变换为标准高斯，即令 \(z=\sqrt{\beta^2 J^2 q}\hat{z}\)，则 \(z\sim\mathcal{N}(0,1)\)，则有

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \exp \left[ \frac{1}{2}\beta^2 J^2 q \left(\sum_{\alpha} S^\alpha \right)^2\right]&=\int_C \frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\sqrt{2\pi}} \exp\left(-\frac{z^2}{2}\right)\exp\left(\beta J\sqrt{q}z\sum_{\alpha} S^\alpha\right)\\ &=\int_C Dz\exp\left(\beta J\sqrt{q}z\sum_{\alpha} S^\alpha\right) \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

其中，$Dz=z(-z^2/2)/ $ 是标准高斯测度. 因此有

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} &\frac{1}{n} \log \mathrm{Tr~} \exp \left(L*\right)\\ &=\frac{1}{n} \log \left\{ \exp\left( -\frac{\beta^2 J^2 qn}{2} \right) \mathrm{Tr~}\exp \left[ \frac{1}{2}\beta^2 J^2 q \left(\sum_{\alpha} S^\alpha \right)^2+\beta\left(J_0 m+h\right) \sum_\alpha S^\alpha \right] \right\}\\ &=\frac{1}{n} \log \left\{ \exp\left( -\frac{\beta^2 J^2 qn}{2} \right) \mathrm{Tr~}\int_C Dz\exp\left(\beta J\sqrt{q}z\sum_{\alpha} S^\alpha+\beta\left(J_0 m+h\right) \sum_\alpha S^\alpha\right)\right\} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

对于上式最后一项，有

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} &\mathrm{Tr~}\int_C Dz\exp\left(\beta J\sqrt{q}z\sum_{\alpha} S^\alpha+\beta\left(J_0 m+h\right) \sum_\alpha S^\alpha\right)\\ &=\int_C Dz\mathrm{~Tr~}\exp\left[\sum_{\alpha} S^\alpha\Big(\beta J\sqrt{q}z+\beta\left(J_0 m+h\right)\Big)\right]\\ &=\int_C Dz \prod_{\alpha=1}^n\mathrm{Tr~}\exp\left[S^\alpha\Big(\beta J\sqrt{q}z+\beta\left(J_0 m+h\right)\Big)\right]\\ &=\int_C Dz \prod_{\alpha=1}^n\mathrm{Tr~}\exp\left[S^\alpha\beta\hat{H}(z)\right] \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

其中，\(\hat{H}(z):=J\sqrt{q}z+\left(J_0 m+h\right)\).
注意到上式最后一步连乘中的每一项都是同样对 \(\{ S^\alpha =\pm 1 \}\) 求和，因此连乘可以转为指数 \[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \prod_{\alpha=1}^n\mathrm{Tr~}\exp\left[S^\alpha\beta\hat{H}(z)\right]&=\prod_{\alpha=1}^n \left\{ \exp\Big(-\beta\hat{H}(z)\Big) +\exp\Big(\beta\hat{H}(z)\Big) \right\}\\ &=\left\{ 2\cosh\Big(\beta\hat{H}(z)\Big) \right\}^n=\exp\left\{n\log \left[2\cosh\Big(\beta\hat{H}(z)\Big) \right]\right\} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

\(\log \mathrm{Tr~} \exp \left(L*\right)/n\) 最终写为

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} &\frac{1}{n} \log \mathrm{Tr~} \exp \left(L*\right)\\ &=\frac{1}{n} \log \left\{ \exp\left( -\frac{\beta^2 J^2 qn}{2} \right) \int Dz \exp\left\{n\log \left[2\cosh\Big(\beta\hat{H}(z)\Big) \right] \right\}\right\}\\ &=\frac{1}{n} \log \int Dz~ \exp\left\{n\log \left[2\cosh\Big(\beta\hat{H}(z)\Big)\right] -\frac{n}{2}\beta^2 J^2 q \right\} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

当 \(n\to 0\)时，利用麦克劳林展开

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \frac{1}{n} \log \mathrm{Tr~} \exp \left(L*\right) &\approx \frac{1}{n} \log \left\{1+n \int D z~ \log\left[ 2\cosh\Big(\beta\hat{H}(z)\Big)\right]-\frac{n}{2} \beta^2 J^2 q\int D z\right\}\\ &\approx \int D z~ \log\left[ 2 \cosh \Big(\beta\hat{H}(z)\Big)\right] -\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 J^2 q \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

其中，第一步是利用指数函数在 \(0\) 附近的展开，第二步是利用对数函数在 \(1\) 附近的展开.
由此，\([\log Z]\) 写为

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} [\log Z] &=N\left\{\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}\left(1+q^2\right)-\frac{\beta J_0}{2} m^2+\lim _{n \rightarrow 0} \left\{\int D z~ \log\left[ 2 \cosh \Big(\beta\hat{H}(z)\Big)\right] -\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 J^2 q\right\}\right\}\\ &=N\left\{\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}\left(1-q^2\right)-\frac{\beta J_0}{2} m^2+\int D z~ \log\left[ 2 \cosh \Big(\beta\hat{H}(z)\Big)\right]\right\} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

根据

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} &\frac{\partial}{\partial m}[\log Z]= -\beta J_0m+\int \mathrm{D} z(\tanh \beta \tilde{H}(z)) \cdot \beta J_0=0\\ &\frac{\partial}{\partial q}[\log Z]= \frac{\beta^2 J^2}{2}(q-1)+\int \mathrm{D} z(\tanh \beta \tilde{H}(z)) \cdot \frac{\beta J}{2 \sqrt{q}} z=0 \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

得

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} &m=\int D z \tanh \beta \tilde{H}(z)\\ &q=1-\int D z \operatorname{sech}^2 \beta \tilde{H}(z)=\int D z \tanh ^2 \beta \tilde{H}(z) \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

3. Phase diagram

考虑 \(h=0\) 的情况，有

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} &m=\int D z \tanh \left( \beta J\sqrt{q}z+ \beta J_0 m \right) \\ &q=\int D z \tanh ^2 \beta \left( \beta J\sqrt{q}z+ \beta J_0 m \right) \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

数值求解方程的不动点可以分别得到 \(q\) 和 \(m\) 的相图

将他们组合在一起即可得到SK模型复本对称解的相图

数值方法的代码为

除了自旋玻璃相与铁磁相的分界线外，另外两条分界线可以通过微扰的方法进行分析。

\(J_0=0\) 时，自由能为 \[ \begin{equation} \beta [f] = - \frac{[\log Z]}{N} = -\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}(1-q)^2 - \int Dz\log 2 \cosh (\beta J \sqrt{q} z) \end{equation} \] 对 \(q\) 做小量展开，有 \[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \beta [f] &= -\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}(1-q)^2 - \int Dz\log 2 \cosh (\beta J \sqrt{q} z) \\ &=-\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}(1-q)^2 - \int Dz\log 2 \left( 1 + \frac{1}{2}(\beta J \sqrt{q} z)^2 + \frac{1}{4!} (\beta J \sqrt{q} z)^4 + \mathcal{O}\left(q^3\right)\right) \\ &=-\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}(1-q)^2 - \log 2 - \frac{1}{2}\beta^2 J^2q \int Dz z^2 - \frac{1}{4!}\beta^4 J^4q^2 \int Dz z^4 \\ &=-\frac{1}{4}\beta^2 J^2 + \frac{1}{2}\beta^2 J^2q - \frac{1}{4}\beta^2 J^2q^2 - \log 2 - \frac{1}{2}\beta^2 J^2q - \frac{3!}{4!} \beta^4 J^4q^2 + \mathcal{O}\left(q^3\right)\\ &=-\frac{1}{4} \beta^2 J^2-\log 2-\frac{1}{4}\beta^2 J^2\left(1-\beta^2 J^2\right) q^2+\mathcal{O}\left(q^3\right) \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

4. Negative entropy

考虑 \(h=0,\;j_0=0\) 的情况，此时 \(\hat{H}(z)=J\sqrt{q}z\)。当 \(T\to 0\) 即 \(\beta \to \infty\) 时，\(\beta \hat{H}(z)\to \infty\)，因此 \[ q=1-\int D z \operatorname{sech}^2 \beta \tilde{H}(z)\to 1 \] 用参数 \(a\) 来线性化 \(q\)，即 \(q=1-aT\,(a>0)\)。\(a\) 的具体形式由下式确定 \[ aT=\lim _{\beta \rightarrow \infty} \int D z \operatorname{sech}^2 \beta \tilde{H}(z)=\frac{1}{\beta J} \int D z\left(\frac{d}{d z} \tanh \beta J z\right) =\frac{1}{\beta J} \int D z [2 \delta(z)]=\sqrt{\frac{2}{\pi}} \frac{T}{J} \]

这里似乎用了 \[ \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x} \tanh(\beta x) \xrightarrow{\beta \to\infty} 2 \delta(x) \]

即 \(a=\sqrt{\frac{2}{\pi}} /J\)。自由能仍采用 \[ \begin{equation} \beta [f] = - \frac{[\log Z]}{N} = -\frac{\beta^2 J^2}{4}(1-q)^2 - \int Dz\log 2 \cosh (\beta J \sqrt{q} z) \end{equation} \] 将 \(q=1-T\sqrt{\frac{2}{\pi}}/J\) 代入，第一项为 \[ \frac{1}{4} \beta^2 J^2(1-q)^2 = \frac{1}{2\pi} \] 第二项为 \[ \begin{aligned} \int \mathrm{D} z \log 2 \cosh (\beta J \sqrt{q} z) & =2 \int_0^{\infty} D z \log 2 \frac{\exp (-\beta J \sqrt{q} z)+\exp (\beta J \sqrt{q} z)}{2} \\ & \approx 2 \int_0^{\infty} D z \log 2 \frac{\exp (\beta J \sqrt{q} z)}{2} \\ & = 2 \beta J \sqrt{q} \int_0^{\infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}} z e^{-z^2/2} \mathrm{d}z \\ & = \sqrt{\frac{2}{\pi}} \beta J \sqrt{q} \end{aligned} \] 将 \(\sqrt{q}\) 展开 \[ \sqrt{q}=\sqrt{1-aT} \approx 1-\frac{1}{2}aT = 1 - \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}} \frac{T}{J} \] 因此 \[ \int \mathrm{D} z \log 2 \cosh (\beta J \sqrt{q} z) = \sqrt{\frac{2}{\pi}} \beta J \left( 1 - \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}} \frac{T}{J} \right) = \sqrt{\frac{2}{\pi}} \beta J-\frac{1}{\pi} \] 可得 \[ \begin{equation} \beta [f] = - \frac{1}{2\pi} - \left( \sqrt{\frac{2}{\pi}} \beta J-\frac{1}{\pi} \right) = -\sqrt{\frac{2}{\pi}} \beta J + \frac{1}{2\pi} \end{equation} \] 即 \[ \begin{equation} \left[ f \right] \approx-\sqrt{\frac{2}{\pi}} J+\frac{T}{2 \pi} \end{equation} \] 根据 \(F=E-TS\) 可以确定基态能量为 \(-\sqrt{2/\pi} J\)，基态熵为 \(S=-\pi/2\)
